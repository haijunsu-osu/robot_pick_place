Task 3: Computer Vision for Pick and Place

Goal

Develop a computer vision system that detects the pose of a wooden box and controls a robot arm (SO-101) to pick and place it.

Vision Setup

  • Wrist-mounted USB camera
  • ChAruCo board for camera calibration and pose estimation
  • Small ArUco marker attached to the wooden block for detection

Motion Planning

  • Use ROS 2 and Moveit for robot arm motion planning and control
  • Integrate vision data to guide the SO-101 arm for pick and place operations
